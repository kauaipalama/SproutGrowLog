Developed from the ground up to be fast, intuative, and private.

Manage plant types

Sprout: Grow log is built around flexibility and ease of use.

Add multiple logs for differant plant types, or create a log for each plant in your garden. It's totally up to you!

Record data

Creating a plant record is fast and easy!

Input ph, conductivity and volume of your nutrient solution in Water/Nutrients, record health on a scale of 1-5, and take a picture in Plant Health.

By the way, everything is optional, almost everything!
So if you're feeling lazy, we still got you covered!

View records

Accessing your data should'nt be a hassle!

View your plant records in a neat and tidy calendar. A highlighted cell indicates a record, and the color, shows the recorded plant health for that respective day.

Easy to read, and most importantly, fun to use!

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to all information collected or submitted on Sprout: Grow Log,
an iOS application developed by Kainoa Palama.

Information we collect:

Access to Sprout does not require an email, username or password. All data is saved and stored within the app’s allotted container. Each app has one container to persist data locally on the device, either through CoreData or directly in NSUserDefaults. Sprout uses your camera and photo library to capture an image. That image is converted to data and stored within a persistence layer built with CoreData. Sprout also stores Strings and Floats in a persistence layer built with CoreData.

Data usage:

Data stored within the device cannot be accessed by the developer(s) of Sprout: Grow Log, nor can the user access or modify this data outside of the app. All data within Sprout: Grow Log is stored locally on the physical device only.

Accessing, modifying, or deleting user’s data:

Accessing and modifying user data can only be done within the app. Deleting user data is done by deleting the app from the device.
